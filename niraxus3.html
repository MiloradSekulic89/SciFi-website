Chapter 3: "The Ship's AI: Nova"

The Nebula Explorer was equipped with a state-of-the-art artificial intelligence system, known as Nova. Nova was responsible for managing the ship's systems and monitoring the crew's health and safety. Nova was a female AI, and her voice was soft and soothing. She was programmed to be helpful and efficient, and the crew had grown to rely on her for everything from navigation to life support. Nova's primary function was to keep a log of the ship's systems and the crew's health and safety. The log was generated automatically and updated in real-time, and it provided a detailed record of the ship's journey.
